HEPARIN FOR CPB

Neuraxial block

Hold medication before procedure : neuraxial blocks should be avoided in a patient with known coagulopathy form any cause.

Restart medication after procedure : 60mins

Hold medication before catheter removal : after normal coagulation restored.

Restart medication after catheter removal : no recommendation.
