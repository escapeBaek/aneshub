APIXABAN(Eliquis)

Neuraxial block

Hold medication before procedure : 72hrs

Restart medication after procedure : at least 6hrs, avoid with indwelling catheters

Hold medication before catheter removal : 26-30hrs or anti-factor Xa assay

Restart medication after catheter removal : 6hrs
