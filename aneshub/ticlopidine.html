TICLOPIDINE(Ticlid)

Neuraxial block

Hold medication before procedure : 10days

Restart medication after procedure : immediately, if no loading dose & 24hrs postop.

Hold medication before catheter removal : no information available.

Restart medication after catheter removal : immediately, if no loading dose & 24hrs postop.

Deep plexus/deep peripheral block

Hold medication before procedure : 10days

Restart medication after procedure : immediately, if no loading dose & 24hrs postop.

Hold medication before catheter removal : no information available.

Restart medication after catheter removal : immediately, if no loading dose & 24hrs postop.

Superficial peripheral block

For patients undergoing other plexus or peripheral techniques, we suggest management(performance, catheter maintenance, and catheter removal) based on site compressibility, vascularity, and consequences of bleeding, should it occur.
